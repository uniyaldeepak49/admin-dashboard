 FAQ Accordion Frequently asked questions How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your email.

How can I update my profile information?

Navigate to your profile page by clicking on your avatar in the top right corner, then select "Profile" from the dropdown menu. You can edit your information there.

What are the system requirements?

This application works on modern web browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. JavaScript must be enabled for full functionality.

How do I contact support?

You can contact our support team through the help section in the application or send an email to {{ 'support@adminpro.com' }}. We typically respond within 24 hours.
